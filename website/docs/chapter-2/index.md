# Chapter 2: Basics of Humanoid Robotics

## Overview

This chapter covers the fundamentals of humanoid robotics, including the design principles, kinematics, dynamics, and control systems that enable these robots to mimic human-like movements and behaviors. We'll explore the challenges and opportunities in creating robots that can interact with human environments.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:
- Explain the design principles behind humanoid robots
- Understand the kinematics and dynamics of humanoid systems
- Describe the main components of a humanoid robot
- Identify the challenges in humanoid robot control and locomotion

## Prerequisites

Before starting this chapter, you should have:
- Basic understanding of physics and mechanics
- Familiarity with Chapter 1 concepts on Physical AI
- Understanding of basic programming concepts

## Estimated Time

This chapter should take approximately 60-75 minutes to complete.
