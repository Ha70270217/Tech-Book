# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

## Overview

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI, exploring the intersection between artificial intelligence and physical systems. We'll examine how AI systems can interact with and control physical environments, from robotics to embodied intelligence.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:
- Define Physical AI and distinguish it from traditional AI approaches
- Understand the key challenges in creating AI systems that interact with the physical world
- Identify the core components of a Physical AI system
- Recognize the potential applications and implications of Physical AI

## Prerequisites

Before starting this chapter, you should have:
- Basic understanding of AI and machine learning concepts
- Familiarity with programming concepts
- Understanding of fundamental physics principles (optional but helpful)

## Estimated Time

This chapter should take approximately 45-60 minutes to complete.
